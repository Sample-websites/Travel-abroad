To be eligible for a PGWP, you must have completed a program of at least 8 months in duration at a recognized institution. You must also apply within 180 days of receiving your final transcript.

3. Apply Online

You can apply for a work permit online through the official website of Immigration, Refugees, and Citizenship Canada (IRCC). Make sure all documents are prepared and submitted as per the requirements.

4. Transition to a Permanent Work Visa

If you are employed in Canada and wish to stay permanently, you can apply for permanent residency through various programs like the Express Entry or Provincial Nominee Program (PNP).

Conclusion

Transferring from a student visa to a work visa in Canada is a straightforward process if you meet the eligibility requirements and follow the proper steps.
